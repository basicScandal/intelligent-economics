  <div class="exp-step">

      <div class="exp-step__desc">Randomly select 50 citizens using existing voter registration rolls. Convene quarterly to review the city's MIND scores and deliberate on value alignment for major policy proposals. Livestream the sessions.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
