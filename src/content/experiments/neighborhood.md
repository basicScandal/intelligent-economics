<div class="exp-panel__steps reveal reveal-delay-2">
  <div class="exp-panel__steps-header">How to Run It</div>
  <div class="exp-step">
    <div class="exp-step__n">1</div>
    <div class="exp-step__content">
      <div class="exp-step__title">Recruit 5 block captains</div>
      <div class="exp-step__desc">One per 200 residents. They collect raw data: business activity, community events, social connections, demographic diversity, air quality, green space access.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="exp-step">
    <div class="exp-step__n">2</div>
    <div class="exp-step__content">
      <div class="exp-step__title">Run the baseline MIND survey</div>
      <div class="exp-step__desc">We provide a ready-to-use survey instrument. Monthly check-ins, quarterly deep assessments. Share results publicly on a neighborhood dashboard we help you build.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="exp-step">
    <div class="exp-step__n">3</div>
    <div class="exp-step__content">
      <div class="exp-step__title">Find the binding constraint</div>
      <div class="exp-step__desc">In a multiplicative system, the lowest MIND capital determines the ceiling. Diagnose it: is it Network (social isolation)? Diversity (economic monoculture)? Material (infrastructure gaps)?</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="exp-step">
    <div class="exp-step__n">4</div>
    <div class="exp-step__content">
      <div class="exp-step__title">Implement one geometry change</div>
      <div class="exp-step__desc">Not a program — a structural change. A weekly skill-share market. A neighborhood investment pool. A tool library. Something that changes the incentive geometry, not just the activity level.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="exp-step">
    <div class="exp-step__n">5</div>
    <div class="exp-step__content">
      <div class="exp-step__title">Publish the results</div>
      <div class="exp-step__desc">Every pilot publishes its full methodology, data, and outcomes to the open repository. You become a case study. Other neighborhoods replicate. The network grows.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
