One priest. Five engineering graduates. One Basque village. Today: 80,000 worker-owners, €12B revenue, 150 countries. Through the 2008 crisis, when Spain's unemployment hit 27%, Mondragon's stayed near zero — workers voted to cut salaries rather than shed colleagues. Different geometry, different outcome. Every time.

**The lesson:** Structural design, not moral superiority. The concentric rings — equal distribution of ownership — produce resilience automatically.
