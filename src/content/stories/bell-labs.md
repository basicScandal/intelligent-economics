AT&amp;T funded Bell Labs as a zone explicitly insulated from quarterly returns. The results: the transistor, information theory, the laser, Unix, cellular networks. Not because researchers were smarter — because the <em>geometry</em> was different. Biologists next to physicists next to mathematicians. Serendipitous collisions were structural, not accidental.

**The lesson:** Change the geometry of incentives inside a protected zone and you change what gets produced. The Guardian Lattice is Bell Labs at civilizational scale.
