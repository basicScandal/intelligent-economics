Welcome to the movement.

You just signed up for Intelligent Economics, and we want to be direct about what that means. This isn't a newsletter. It's not a think tank mailing list. You've joined a growing group of people who believe economic measurement is fundamentally broken -- and who are doing something about it.

**The problem is simple:** GDP measures transactions, not progress. A country can have rising GDP while its education collapses, its ecosystems degrade, and its institutions erode. We've been flying blind for decades.

**The MIND framework is the fix.** MIND stands for:

- **Material** -- Physical infrastructure, natural resources, environmental health
- **Intelligence** -- Education quality, AI capability, research output, knowledge systems
- **Network** -- Social cohesion, institutional trust, connectivity, civic engagement
- **Diversity** -- Cultural richness, biodiversity, economic variety, resilience

Prosperity = Material x Intelligence x Network x Diversity. If any dimension hits zero, the whole equation collapses. That's not a bug -- it's the most important feature. It means you can't fake prosperity by maxing one dimension while ignoring others.
