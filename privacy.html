Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

What we collect

When you submit a form on this site, we collect the information you provide: name, email address, location, area of expertise, availability, selected roles, and any optional message. We also record the date and time of submission.

How we use it

Your information is used solely to:

  • Contact you about volunteer opportunities matching your skills
  • Send project updates (if you opt in)
  • Aggregate anonymous statistics about our volunteer base (e.g., "40% of volunteers are economists")

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Form processing

Form submissions are processed by FormSubmit.co, which delivers submissions to our email. FormSubmit.co's privacy policy governs their handling of data in transit. We do not store form data in a separate database at this time.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-respecting analytics service that:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track users across sites
  • Is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR

Cookies

This site does not set any cookies.

Third-party services

This site loads resources from:

  • jsDelivr CDN — Three.js and GSAP libraries
  • Fontshare — Web fonts (Clash Display, Satoshi)
  • Plausible — Privacy-respecting analytics
  • FormSubmit.co — Form submission processing

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data from our records
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email Rob@theoradical.ai.
